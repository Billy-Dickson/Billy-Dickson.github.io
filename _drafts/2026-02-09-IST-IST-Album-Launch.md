I finished then got changed and headed to the City Cafe to get some food (Nacho's Yum!), followed by a quick trundle down the road to [Bannerman's](https://www.bannermanslive.co.uk/) to meet the guys, we had an hour so, so I settled in for a yatter and a few beers as you do. We headed off to see the band quite sharp, as there was no support band then they were promoting their new Album "Dagger", having heard "Dagger" it a few times at that point, I was quite looking forward to seeing it performed live.

I've been following this band for a couple of years now, ever since my friend [Danny Gig-antics](https://www.gig-antics.live) suggested them to me (he knows me well).

## The Gig

The Venue - [The Caves Edinburgh](https://unusualvenuesedinburgh.com/)
